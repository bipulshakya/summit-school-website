Principal's Message

From The Principal

Prem Bahadur Thapa — Principal

"

Dear Students, Parents and Well-wishers,

It is with great pride and humility that I welcome you to Summit English Boarding School — a place where education meets inspiration. Our mission has always been to go beyond textbooks, to nurture young minds with critical thinking, creativity, and compassion.

At Summit, we believe that every child holds extraordinary potential. Our dedicated educators work tirelessly to create an environment where learning is joyful, curiosity is celebrated, and character is built alongside academic achievement. Together, let us build a bright future for our children — one that is worthy of their dreams.

P

Prem Bahadur Thapa

Principal — Summit English Boarding School

Chairman's Message

From The Chairman

Shree Ram Adhikari — Chairman

"

Dear Community Members,

Summit English Boarding School was founded with a single vision: to make quality education accessible and transformative for every child in our community. Since our establishment in 2050 BS, we have remained unwavering in that commitment.

Education is the most powerful instrument for change. We have built Summit School not just as an institution, but as a community — where students, teachers, parents, and staff collaborate to achieve something far greater than any one of us could achieve alone. My promise to you is that we will continue to invest in our teachers, infrastructure, and programs — because your children deserve nothing less than the very best.

S

Shree Ram Adhikari

Chairman — Summit English Boarding School
